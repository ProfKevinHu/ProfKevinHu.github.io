Beyond the Incenter

Beyond the Incenter is an upcoming Olympiad geometry guide aimed at the BMO2/USAMO level. It focuses on the structural elegance of the triangle, covering projective methods, complete quadrilaterals, trigonometric approaches, and harmonic configurations without relying on computational algebra.
